# Greylyn Wayne — Home Staging & Interior Design

> Portland, Oregon's trusted home staging and interior design experts since 2015.

## About
Greylyn Wayne is a family-run interior design and home staging company based in Portland, Oregon. Founded by Jody Wallace, the company specializes in custom design and staging solutions that accentuate the character of a space. We serve homeowners, realtors, builders, and property managers throughout Oregon and Southwest Washington.

## Services
- **Home Staging**: Occupied staging, vacant staging, luxury estate staging, new construction & model home staging
- **Interior Design**: Full-service design, consultations, spatial planning, color & material selection, custom furniture sourcing
- **Furniture Rental**: High-end furniture rentals for staging, model units, and commercial spaces
- **Short-Term Rental Design**: Airbnb and vacation rental design to maximize bookings and guest experience
